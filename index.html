The Autistic Revolution team are hard at work creating an international digital publication, aiming to share and celebrate Autistic and neurodivergent life experiences from all walks of life!

We are firmly committed to empowering our neurodivergent kin through the use of affirming language, readiness to listen, and compassionate values. If you are neurodivergent, we warmly invite you to join us!

You don't need to be a seasoned writer, artist, or creator to contribute to Autistic Revolution; we only ask that you share our commitment to a better world.
